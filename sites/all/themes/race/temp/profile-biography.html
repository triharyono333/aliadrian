Ali Adrian Rusmiputro

Team INDONESIA TEAM
Date of Birth 29 September 1993

Favourite Racer Benny Hidayat, Jorge Lorenzo
Favourite Circuit Circuito de Almeria
Website http://www.aliadrian.com

Facebook Ali Adrian (athlete)
Youtube Ali Adrian
 

Ali Adrian Rusmiputro was born in Jakarta, Indonesia. At 21 years of age, he has become a young talented racing star. He started his career at the age of 3, when his father gave him a pocket-bike 50cc, as a present. At that particular moment it was just a game for him.

At the age of 9, he took up motocross, a kind of motorcycling sport recommended to him. It is a good way to acquire ability and control, as well as to become a rider.

Being fourteen and mature enough, he decided, together with his family, that it was time to start a new phase of his of his life. That is, to compete and work hard so as to make his dream come true.

Ali Adrian's life then started to change, where he had to work hard and do his best. It was not really easy for a person at his age. He left home and his family in order to live in another city, Yogyakarta, and took part in the National Championship of Indonesia Motorprix (Underbone). During his first year he had to live in the team's headquarter.

His results were very promising as at his first race in Underbone 150cc series. Soon after that, his talent starts to attract people's attention.

At the end of the season 2012, Adrian decided to go to Spain in order to develop his skills and became an official rider of the World Championship events. His path to the professional motor racing world was soon become wide open.

Ali Adrian has now become the youngest Indonesian rider who is considered to be the next winner of the highest ladder of the World Championship, the MOTOGP series.

Currently Adrian is represented by David Garcia, the owner of International Circuito de Almeria - Spain, as well as the sports manager of Tito Rabat (Spain), who is the World Champion of MOTO2 and MOTO3 series. Adrian is the first and only Asian rider who is being contracted personally by David Garcia.

Ali Adrian is going to set his sail to become the International professional rider and we shall see the best of his achievement. With humble wishes and strong will, Adrian hereby needs major support from the people of Indonesia, as well from the potential sponsors and the Government.
