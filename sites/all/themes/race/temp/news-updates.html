Meet & Greet with Ali Adrian Rusmiputro & David Garcia

by Admin

Rider Ali Adrian Rusmiputro will represent Indonesia at the Moto2 European Championship event, part of the 2015 FIM CEV Spanish Road Racing Championship, riding a 650cc bike.
The 21-year-old Adrian has already made a name for himself on the Indonesian motorbike racing circuit, having competed in many national competitions since he was 14 years old, after first developing an interest in racing bikes when he was 3 years old.

The Promising Seed

by Admin

Indonesia's young motor racing upstart, Ali Adrian Rusmiputro is about to reach one of the highest pinnacles of his career — he is set to compete in the Moto 2 European Championships event of the 2015 FIM CEV Spanish Motorbike Championships. He means business when he grabs the chequered flag in the International Promotion Bike Race, in Almeria, Spain, on January 23, 2015. Riding the Honda CBR 600 mean machine, Adrian shows the world that he is more than serious.
